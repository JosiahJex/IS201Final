About Me


A brief explanation about who I am and some of the things I enjoy

My Favorite Chess Openings
  1. The Italian Game
    • A classic and aggressive opening for white
    • Focuses on quick development and center control
    • My favorite opening when playing as white
  2. The Scandinavian Defense
    • Black immediately challenges white's center pawn on move one
    • Simple and straightforward, easy to learn and play
    • A solid and reliable choice against e4
  3. The Sicilian Defense
    • One of the most popular responses to e4
    • Black fights for the center in an indirect way
    • Used by many world champions throughout history
